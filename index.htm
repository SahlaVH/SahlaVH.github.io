Schezwan Chana Veggie Wrap

  • Time: 15 mins
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Chana Masala (Dry)1tbsp
Schezwan Sauce1tsp
Grated Veggie 1/4cup

Step 1:First Heat Oil In A Pan. Add Ginger Garlic Paste. Fry For Few Secs. Add All Veggies Except Spring Onion. Fry Little So That The Crunchiness Of Veggies Should Remain Intact. Add Soya Sauce, Salt, Pepper Powder, Sugar.

Step 2:Mix Curd With Mayonnaise. Now Assemble All Ingredients.

Step 3:Take A Roti/Chapati. Spread Schezwan Sauce. Then Curd Mayo Mixture Over It.

Step 4:Then Spread Dry Chana Masala (I Had Some Left Over Chana Masala) Over This. Now Put Fried Veggies Over Chana Masala. Sprinkle Chat Masala & Roasted Jeera Powder. Also Put Some Sliced Onions Over It.

Corn And Cheese Balls Twin Wrap

  • Time: 50 mins
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Milk1/2 cup
Grated Sweet Corn1 Cup
Grated Cheese,maida1 /2cup

Step 1:Heat Tawa And Toast Roties Both Side By Applying Some Oil And Keep Aside Take Pan Boil Milk Ok Add Cheese Grated Sweet Corn Green Chilli Salt Pepper Cook Till It Leaves The Edges Take It Out From The Pan Keep Aside For Cooling And Make A Salary Of Maida And Water

Step 2:Make 12 Balls From This Mixture , Dip This Ball In Maida And Water Slurry Roll On Bread Crumbs And Fry Them Hot Oil Take It Out Golden And Crisp. Take A Big Bowl And Chopped Tomatoes Chopped Onion Chopped Bell Paper Oregano Salt Pepper Green Chilli, Oregano Seasoning And Mix Well. And Keep Aside For Later Use

Step 3: For Assembling Take A Besan Cheela Apply One Tablespoon Schezwan Sauce On It Now Take A Roti And Apply Mayonnaise On It Cover Roti With Besan Cheela Put One Lettuce Leaf On Besan Cheela

Step 4: Now Add Add Two Tablespoon Salsa On Lettuce Leaf Now Add 3 Cheese And And Sweet Corn Balls Over The Salsa And Press Them Lightly Spread Some Mayonnaise And Schezwan Sauce Over Sweet Corn Balls Now Roll It Properly Besan And Roti Wrap. Attach With Toothpick Make All All Wraps Like This And Sarve Them Immediately.

Masala Pasta Frankie

  • Time: 25 mins
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta(Boiled)1 Cup
Maggi Masala Powder1/2 tspn
Slices Veggies1 cup

Step 1: Heat Oil In A Pan,Add Chopped Onion,Green Chillies Saute For A Minute.Add Chopped Carrot,French Beans Cook For 2 Minutes.Add Tomato Choppe,Salt,Turmeric Powder Mix Well Agian Cook For A Minute.Now Add Boiled Pasta Mix Well,Add Maggi Masala Cook For A Minute And Stire Well. Now Pasta Filling Is Ready.

Step 2:Make A Dough Of Wheat Flour,Salt And Water.Take A Small Portion From The Dough.Now Roll A Round Circle Chapati.Heat A Tawa,Place The Chapati And Cook It With Oil. Now Take A Chapati/Roti And Spread Tomato Sauce And Add The Pasta Filling The Middle Of The Chapati,Add Slices Veggies Salad,Sprinkle Maggi Masala.

Step 3: Now Roll It.Wrap Bottom Of The Roll With Butter Paper And Seal The Bottom.Repeat The Process To Make Remaining Rolls. Now Frankie Pasta Roll Is Ready.
